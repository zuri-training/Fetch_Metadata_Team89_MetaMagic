About Us

Metamagic is an online tool that collects and displays metadata
of different uploaded file types with a reliable database system
that allows storage and export of these files.

Uploading and saving of files and data on the internet comes in
handy in our everyday life, notwithstanding, it exposes
sensitive, private details, and compromises our privacy as we go
about accomplishing our tasks.

Metamagic does not only perform the primary function of
processing metadata, it also keeps the user details confidential,
guarding against cyber attack, not compromising privacy and
exports metadata in a snap when required, just like magic!
